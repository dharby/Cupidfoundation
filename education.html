Education

Scholarships, learning centres, and supplies that put children on a path to choice and agency.

Our approach

How we make education last.

We believe education is the first infrastructure of freedom. Too many children in underserved communities drop out not for lack of ability, but for lack of a desk, a book, or a meal to focus on. Cupid Foundation removes those barriers.

From full scholarships to after-school learning centres, our education work is built with schools and parents, not dropped in from outside. Every cohort is tracked for retention, learning gains, and transition to the next level.

Programs

What we run in education.

Scholarship Fund

Full and partial scholarships covering fees, uniforms, and exam costs for girls and boys at risk of dropping out.

Learning Centres

Community-run study hubs with tutors, internet, and libraries in settlements without nearby schools.

Back-to-School Kits

Books, bags, and supplies distributed each term so cost is never the reason a child stays home.

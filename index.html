About Cupid Foundation

Born in Nigeria, built for the people we serve.

Our mission: to unlock the transformative power of education, standing united against the scourge of illiteracy by breaking down barriers and empowering every individual to reach their full potential.

Our vision: a future Africa of our true dreams, a continent brimming with possibilities and boundless opportunities, where education is the cornerstone of positive change, sustainable development, and a healthier, more prosperous world for all.

Our Journey

A decade of quiet, persistent impact.

2014

Founded in Ibadan

Started with a community education outreach serving children in a single neighbourhood.

2017

Healthcare programs launch

First free medical outreach; 10+ patients seen across partner communities.

2020

Emergency response

Rapid relief and food security for vulnerable households across 50+ communities.

2023

Skills & economic empowerment

Launched vocational academies; 20+ youths trained and 5+ small businesses supported.

2024

Scholarships & learning materials

Awards and education materials for secondary school students; 20+ scholarships and 50+ children supported.

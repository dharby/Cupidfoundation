Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, donation details) and limited analytics data. We use it to process donations, send communications you opt into, and improve our work. We never sell your data. Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant processors; we do not store card numbers.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@cupidfoundation.org. We retain records as required by Nigerian law and for audit transparency.

This site uses essential cookies and, with consent, analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy.

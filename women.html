Women Empowerment

Enterprise grants, financial literacy, and advocacy for women to lead and provide.

Our approach

How we make women empowerment last.

When a woman earns, a household eats, children stay in school, and a community rises. We back women with the capital, skills, and voice to build livelihoods and claim their place in decision-making.

Our women’s work pairs small enterprise grants with financial literacy and peer cohorts, because isolation, not ability, is often the real barrier.

What we run in women empowerment.

Enterprise Grants

Seed grants and starter kits for women launching trading, farming, and making businesses.

Financial Literacy

Practical training in budgeting, saving, and managing a small enterprise.

Advocacy & Voice

Cohorts that organise for land, inheritance, and representation rights.
